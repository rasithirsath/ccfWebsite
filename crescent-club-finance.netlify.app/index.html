Welcome to CCF

The Crescent Club of Finance, a new initiative focused on providing students with a comprehensive club for exploring various facets of finance, is brought to your attention. This initiative encompasses educational events, networking opportunities, and hands-on experiences

Holistic Skill Development

To equip both finance and non-finance students with a well-rounded understanding of financial concepts through interactive workshops, simulations, and practical experiences.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

To promote cross-disciplinary collaboration by facilitating projects and events that bring together students from various academic backgrounds to apply financial principles in real world scenarios
