Finvibe Fiesta

"Finvibe Fiesta is an exhilarating journey in the world of finance with our upcoming event - FINVIBE Fiesta 2024! Finvibe Fiesta is focused on the pivotal decision of whether to invest or save money for the future, the event unfolds in two dynamic parts, promising a unique and enriching experience for participants."

Level 1: No specific regulations govern team participation during this initial round. Teams are encouraged to showcase their skills and creativity freely.

Level 2: Advancement to Round 2 is exclusive to the participant teams from Round 1. This round aims to filter and highlight the top-performing teams.

Level 3:The third round involves the participation of the top 25% of teams from Round 2, those who did not qualify for the final round. This stage introduces an auction-style setting, adding a strategic element to the competition

Level 4: The pinnacle of the event, Round 4 features a head-to-head competition between the top team from Round 2, determining the ultimate champion.

The Finvibe Fiesta: Learn finance in the fun way.

Finvibe Fiesta is a captivating event designed to coincide with Financial Literature Week, aiming to elevate financial awareness among students.

Level 1

No specific regulations govern team participation during this initial round. Teams are encouraged to showcase their skills and creativity freely.

Level 2

Advancement to Round 2 is exclusive to the participant teams from Round 1. This round aims to filter and highlight the top-performing teams.

Level 3

The third round involves the participation of the top 25% of teams from Round 2, those who did not qualify for the final round. This stage introduces an auction-style setting, adding a strategic element to the competition

Final Round

The pinnacle of the event, Round 4 features a head-to-head competition between the top team from Round 2, determining the ultimate champion.
